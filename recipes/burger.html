Burger

A smash burger is a thin, crispy beef burger made by pressing a ball of ground beef onto a hot pan or griddle. Smashing creates a caramelized crust, giving the burger a rich, beefy flavor with crispy edges.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (80/20 works best)
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese slices (American or cheddar)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Oil or butter

Steps

  1. Heat a pan or griddle on high heat.
  2. Divide ground beef into small balls.
  3. Place a beef ball on the hot pan.
  4. Smash it flat using a spatula.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Cook until crispy on the bottom.
  7. Flip and add cheese on top.
  8. Toast the buns lightly.
  9. Assemble the burger and serve hot.
