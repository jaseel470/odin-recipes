Neapolitan pizza

Description

Neapolitan pizza is a traditional Italian pizza from Naples, made with a soft, thin crust and simple toppings. It is baked at very high temperatures, giving it a light, airy texture with slightly charred edges. Classic toppings include tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, olive oil, and basil.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to the highest temperature possible.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a thin round shape.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Add pieces of fresh mozzarella on top.
  5. Sprinkle a little salt and drizzle olive oil.
  6. Bake the pizza until the crust is puffed and lightly charred.
  7. Remove from the oven and add fresh basil leaves.
  8. Slice and serve hot.
