Shawarma

Home

Description

Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern street food made from thinly sliced meat—usually chicken, beef, or lamb—stacked on a rotating spit and slowly roasted.

The meat is shaved off, wrapped in flatbread, and typically served with garlic sauce, pickles, and vegetables

Ingredients

  1. Chiken or Beef
  2. flatbread or Pita
  3. Garlic
  4. Yogurt
  5. Lemon juice
  6. Oil
  7. Salt
  8. Black pepper

      9. Filling

    1. Tomato
    2. Onion
    3. Garlic sauce

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken (or meat) into thin strips
  2. In a bowl, mix yogurt, crushed garlic, lemon juice, oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add the meat to the mixture and mix well.
  4. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes (longer = better taste).
  5. Heat a pan and cook the meat until fully cooked and slightly browned.
  6. Warm the flatbread or pita.
  7. Place the cooked meat on the bread.
  8. Add sliced onion and tomato.
  9. Roll the bread tightly and serve
